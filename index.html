    Garuda corporate business solutions

    Garuda corporate business solutions was established in 2006 as MIMA assurance and is located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. We became a private limited company as a result of consistent growth and due to our unwavering focus on our customers. With our head office at Technopark, we have 6 branches across Kerala and a dedicated team for 24*7 online service. We have achieved several outstanding performance awards from various insurance companies. We are a total solution provider in the field of insurance, with a specialty in motor vehicle insurance and allied services with more than 17 years of experience and licensed by the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority (IRDA). We, Garuda corporate business solutions private limited, have partnered with various public and private insurance companies and have a technically qualified and competent team of professionals to offer customized solutions to the customers.
